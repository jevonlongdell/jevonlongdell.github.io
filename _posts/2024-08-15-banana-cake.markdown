# Ingredients:
 - 200g sugar
 - 250g milk
 - 70g butter
 - 2 ripe mashed bananas
 - 290g self raising flour
 - 4 eggs

# Directions:
  1. Turn on oven to 180C
  2. Prepare tin
  3. Melt butter 
  4. Mix all ingredients and beat for 3min
  5. Bake for 30min +
