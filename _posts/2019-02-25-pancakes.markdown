# Ingredients:

*    250g of flour, mostly wholemeal
*    3 eggs
*    large dollup of olive oil
*    big pinch of salt
*    enough soymilk/water to make it the right consistency (c. 600ml)


# Directions:

1. Mix it all together together.

2. Cook a ladleful at a time in frypan.
