# Ingredients:

* 1 large tin of tuna (425g)
* 1 cup rolled oats
* 3 eggs
* 1 onion
* 2 garlic cloves
* herbs (tyme,sage,chives)
* salt and pepper
* dollup of olive oil

# Directions:

1. Beat the egs

2. Comine everything together, maybe add a bit of extra liquid

3. Fry in lots of olive oil.
